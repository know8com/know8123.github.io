ABOUT US
Know8 is a college socializing platform that provides students with a whole new experience of learning new things. Not only they get to learn new technologies but if anyone has some knowledge about anything then we will give people like them a platform for organizing several events in college to build up their self-confidence and they will also get to spread their seeds of wisdom to others as well.
